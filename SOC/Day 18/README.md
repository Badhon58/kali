## What is Threat Intelligence ?

- _Threat Intelligence (TI)_ : knowledge about adversaries, their tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs)
- _TTPs_ : Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures.

- _Helps Answer_:
  - Who is attacking?
  - What are they doing ?
  - Why are they targeting us ?
  - How do we defend ?
- SOC analysts rely on TI for context while investigating alerts.

## Types of Threat Intelligence

- _Strategic TI_ : High level, long-term trends (CISOs, decision makers).
- _Tactical TI_ : TTPs from frameworks like MITRE ATT&CK.
- _Operational TI_ : Campaign-level details (malware used, target sector).
- _Technical TI_ : IPs, domains, hashes (day-to-day SOC use)
