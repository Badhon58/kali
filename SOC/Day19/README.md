# Threat Intelligence Platform

## Anomali ThreatStream

_Purpose_ : Anomali ThreatStream is a Threat Intelligence Platform (TIP) that aggregates, normalize, and enriches threat data from multiple sources. It enables SOC analysts to operationalize threat intelligence by correlating Indicators of Compromise(IOCs) with internal security data to detect, prevent, and respond to attacks.

- _Key Features_:
  - Threat Data Aggregation
  - Enrichment & Correlation
  - Automation & Orchestration
  - Threat Sharing
  - Machine Learning & Analytics
